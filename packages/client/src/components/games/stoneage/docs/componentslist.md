# Stone Age - Component Inventory

## Boards & Reference
*   **1 Gameboard:** Central board featuring all worker placement zones, resource locations, tracks, and decks.
*   **4 Individual Player Boards:** Personal player boards to manage active tools, constructed huts, and gathered resources.
*   **1 Information Sheet:** Quick reference guide outlining the game phases and final scoring mechanics.

## Wooden Figures & Markers (Meeples)
*   **40 Wooden People:** 10 worker meeples for each of the 4 player colors.
*   **8 Wooden Markers:** 2 scoring/agriculture cylinders per player color for tracking progress on the board.
*   **1 Start Player Figure:** The starting player marker to determine turn order for the round.

## Resources & Sustenance
*   **68 Wooden Resources:** Physical materials divided into Wood, Clay, Stone, and Gold.
*   **53 Food Tiles:** Food tokens of various denominations used to feed the tribe during the feeding phase.

## Progress Elements (Tiles & Cards)
*   **28 Building Tiles:** Hut tiles (both fixed and variable costs) available for players to construct.
*   **36 Civilization Cards:** Progression cards providing immediate bonuses and end-game scoring multipliers.

## Dice & Mechanics
*   **7 Dice:** Standard 6-sided dice used to resolve resource gathering and hunting.
*   **1 Leather Dice Cup:** Cup used for rolling the dice during resource resolution.
